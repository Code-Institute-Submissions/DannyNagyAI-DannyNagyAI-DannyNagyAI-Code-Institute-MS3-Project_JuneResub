Our story

iSandwich was founded in Sundsvall, Sweden in 2021 by Daniel "the Sandwich Guru" Nagy.

The need for a Sandwich community was very important at that time and it is still to this day (and maybe forever).

He promised to help his Sandwich friends to build a fun community on the web and only a few days later... the "iSandwich" was born. Yay!
